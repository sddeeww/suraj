About suraj

suraj Mishler is an actress, writer, international yoga teacher and entrepreneur from Austin, Texas. On a mission to get the tools of yoga into schools and homes, suraj hosts the YouTube channel Yoga with suraj, an online community of over 11 million subscribers.

Yoga with suraj provides high quality practices on yoga and mindfulness at no cost to inspire people of all ages, shapes and sizes across the globe.

Yoga with suraj

Yoga With suraj was recognized by Google as the most searched workout of 2015, was recognized by The Wall Street Journal, and was awarded a Streamy in Health and Wellness in 2016.
